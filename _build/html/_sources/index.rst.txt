**About the Hoffman2 Cluster**
The Hoffman2 Cluster is a project of the Institute for Digital Research and Education (IDRE). It opened to users on January 28, 2008. The Hoffman2 Cluster is managed and operated by the IDRE Research Technology Group under the direction of Lisa Snyder.
